The Infraon Infinity course covers the complete suite of Infraon tools—Asset, ITSM, and NMS—providing users with an all-in-one solution to efficiently manage assets, services, and networks. Participants will explore Asset modules, including IT Asset Lifecycle Management, Software & License Management, and Network Discovery. The ITSM modules cover Incident, Problem, Change, and Service Request Management, while NMS modules focus on Network Monitoring, Fault Management, and Traffic Analysis. Ideal for IT professionals, this course offers practical guidance on delivering streamlined IT services, optimizing resource allocation, and maintaining network performance within a single platform.
